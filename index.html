MY FAVORITE HOBBY

Playing Piano

By: Nicholas Bhasin

What I like about playing piano is that it gives me a choice to playing any genre of music that I want. I can also play sheet music to learn any song without someone having to teach me. I also really like the improvisation side of music since my favorite genre is jazz, and jazz is based on improvisation. In addition, piano and many other instrument have chords which is what the left hand dose on the paino to give a song more sound to it. chords are always satisfying to play at the right time, and hitting them perfectly is a great feeling. The piano is also a nice looking instrument to me, and I think it is the best looking instrument out there. Pianos can also be expensive or cheap since they can come in the traditional acoustic version, or the electric version which is what I have since they are a lot cheaper. Also, acoustic pianos can come in a grand piano form or an upright piano form for homes and apartments to save space. Although, grand pianos typicaly sound better they are more expensive then their upright counterparts. In contrast, electric pianos come in all shapes an sizes, prices and sounds.Electric pianos can make more than just the normal piano sound, it can make any sound you can imagine since you can upload your own set of sounds to an electric piano.
